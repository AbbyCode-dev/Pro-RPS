🎮 How to Play Rock–Paper–Scissors

Rock–Paper–Scissors is a classic hand game played between two players. In this version, you compete against the computer. Choose wisely and try to outsmart your opponent!

📌 Game Objective

Be the first to reach 5 wins before the computer.

🕹 How to Play

  1. Click on one of the three buttons: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  2. The computer will automatically choose its move.
  3. The result of the round will appear instantly.
  4. The score updates after every round.
  5. The first to reach 5 wins becomes the overall winner.

📖 Game Rules

  • 🪨 Rock beats Scissors
  • ✂️ Scissors beats Paper
  • 📄 Paper beats Rock
  • Same move = Tie

🏆 Winning the Game

  • Each win gives you 1 point.
  • Each computer win gives the computer 1 point.
  • Ties do not count as wins.
  • When either player reaches 5 wins, the game ends automatically.
  • You must click Restart Game to play again.

🔄 Restarting the Game

Click the Restart Game button to reset scores and start a new match.
